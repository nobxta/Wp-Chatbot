--- FAQs ---
Q: Is booking advance refundable?
A: 50% of advance refundable if cancelled 15+ days before departure.
   No refund within 7 days of departure.
Q: How much advance to book?
A: 30% of total package cost confirms the booking. Balance 7 days before travel.
Q: Are flights included?
A: No package includes flights unless explicitly mentioned. We can help book
   flights separately at actual cost.
Q: Can packages be customized?
A: Yes! Hotels, duration, and itinerary can all be customized. Share your
   requirements and we will prepare a custom quote within 24 hours.
Q: Payment methods?
A: UPI, bank transfer, credit/debit cards (3% surcharge on cards).
Q: Child policy?
A: Below 5 years free (no extra bed). 5–11 years: 50% of adult cost with extra bed.
